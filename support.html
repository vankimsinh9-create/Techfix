Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my guide after purchase?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with a download link. You can also access your guide anytime by logging into your account on our website.

What if the guide doesn't solve my problem?

If our guide doesn't help you solve your tech issue, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Just contact our support team for a full refund.

Can I get one-on-one help with my tech problem?

Yes! We offer expert support services where you can get direct help from our certified technicians. You can add this service to your guide purchase.

How long does it take to get support?

We typically respond to support requests within 24 hours. For premium support customers, we guarantee a response within 4 hours during business days.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we offer support via email and live chat. Our live chat support is available to premium support customers.
